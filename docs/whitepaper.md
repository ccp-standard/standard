# The Cryptographic Control Plane — Whitepaper

## Publication

**Title:** The Cryptographic Control Plane: A New Architecture for Continuous Cryptographic Evolution

**Author:** ANKATech Solutions INC

**Year:** 2026

## Abstract

The accelerating deprecation of classical cryptographic algorithms, driven by advances in quantum computing and evolving regulatory mandates, exposes a fundamental architectural gap in enterprise systems: the absence of a dedicated layer for managing cryptographic operations as infrastructure. This whitepaper introduces the Cryptographic Control Plane (CCP) — an architectural pattern that decouples cryptographic policy from application logic, enabling organizations to adopt, rotate, and retire algorithms without modifying the systems that depend on them. The paper defines the CAPA framework for assessing organizational cryptographic maturity and establishes minimum capability requirements for conformant CCP implementations.
